Pricing Methodology & Rules

01

Benchmark Protocol

Rolling 3-month average calculated from market diesel prices, rounded to 2 decimal places for precision.

02

Movement Index

Calculated as (Current - Benchmark) / Benchmark. Negative movement is indexed to 0% to prevent rate deflation.

03

Surcharge Engine

Movement percentage is applied strictly to the Fuel Portion (default 25%) of the base rate.

Example: R2,500 base @ 25% fuel (R625). If fuel moves +10%, surcharge = R62.50. Final = R2,562.50.
